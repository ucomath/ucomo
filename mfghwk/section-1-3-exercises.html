Exercises 1.3 Graphs of Functions (Homework 1.3)

¶

In Problems 1–8, use the graphs to answer the questions about the functions.

1.

  1. Find \(h(-3)\text{,}\) \(h(1)\text{,}\) and \(h(3)\text{.}\)

  2. For what value(s) of \(z\) is \(h(z) = 3\text{?}\)

  3. Find the intercepts of the graph. List the function values given by the intercepts.

  4. What is the maximum value of \(h(z)\text{?}\)

  5. For what value(s) of \(z\) does \(h\) take on its maximum value?

  6. On what intervals is the function increasing? Decreasing?

2.

  1. Find \(G(-3)\text{,}\) \(G(-1)\text{,}\) and \(G(2)\text{.}\)

  2. For what value(s) of \(s\) is \(G(s) = 3\text{?}\)

  3. Find the intercepts of the graph. List the function values given by the intercepts.

  4. What is the minimum value of \(G(s)\text{?}\)

  5. For what value(s) of \(s\) does \(G\) take on its minimum value?

  6. On what intervals is the function increasing? Decreasing?

3.

  4. Find the maximum and minimum values of \(R(p)\text{.}\)

  5. For what value(s) of \(p\) does \(R\) take on its maximum and minimum values?

  6. On what intervals is the function increasing? Decreasing?

17.

The graph shows the speed of sound in the ocean as a function of depth, \(S = f (d)\text{.}\) The speed of sound is affected both by increasing water pressure and by dropping temperature. (Source: Scientific American)

  1. Evaluate \(f (1000)\) and explain its meaning.

  2. Solve \(f (d) = 1500\) and explain its meaning.

  3. At what depth is the speed of sound the slowest, and what is the speed? Write your answer with function notation.

  4. Describe the behavior of \(f (d)\) as \(d\) increases.

18.

The graph shows the water level in Lake Superior as a function of time, \(L = f (t)\text{.}\) (Source: The Canadian Hydrographic Service)

  1. Evaluate \(f (1997)\) and explain its meaning.

  2. Solve \(f (t) = 183.5\) and explain its meaning.

  3. In which two years did Lake Superior reach its highest levels, and what were those levels? Write your answers with function notation.

  4. Over which two-year period did the water level drop the most?

  2. Solve \(f (t) = 70\) and explain its meaning.

  3. When did the federal debt reach its highest level since 1960, and what was that level? Write your answer with function notation.

  4. What is the longest time interval over which the federal debt was decreasing?
